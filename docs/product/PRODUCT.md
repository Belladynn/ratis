# Ratis — Product Vision

---

## Mission

Ratis is a grocery list optimisation app that lets users follow an optimised route to pick up each item at the nearby store offering the lowest price.

**Target:** people on a tight budget — families, students, unemployed individuals.

---

## The Promise

By the end of the month, the user has recouped their subscription cost, saved time, and has information that helps them lose less money day to day.

---

## What Ratis Is Not

- Ratis offers cashback but **is not a cashback app**
- Ratis **does not deliver, does not order** — it recommends
- Ratis **is not a store-visit app** — it creates optimised routes based on the user's shopping list
- Ratis **is not partisan** — it shows the lowest prices, full stop. Never greenwashing, never paid promotion

---

## Core Features

Without these four features, Ratis is not Ratis.

**1. Price Comparator**
Compares product prices across nearby stores for the user.

**2. Shopping List Creator and Optimiser**
The user creates their list, Ratis optimises to minimise the total cost.

**3. Route Optimiser**
Generates a multi-store route optimised to pick up each item at the best price.

**4. Frictionless Data Collection — fire and forget**
The user triggers, Ratis does the rest in the background. Three modes:
- Photo of a receipt → automatic OCR
- Electronic price label in store → automatic processing
- Product barcode → automatic resolution

The experience must never be "annoying". Zero friction. Always.

---

## Gamification

Gamification is a **retention and reward** mechanism.

The user has fun while doing their shopping and at the same time enriches our dataset. They earn cabecoins and rewards **only** when they perform actions that enrich our data — scans, corrections, price entries.

It is a virtuous cycle: the user plays → data gets richer → prices become more accurate → the user saves more → they stay.

---

## Business Model

**Primary pillar — Monthly/annual subscription**
Revenue is partly redistributed via gift cards obtained as rewards, and above all via the quality of the service provided.

**Secondary pillar under construction — B2B Analytics**
The aggregated and anonymised data collected through scans represents a valuable asset: price trends, stock-outs, substitution behaviours. This data can be sold to brands and retailers in the form of market insights.

This dimension must be **structured right now** in architectural and DB schema choices — every technical decision must preserve the ability to exploit this data in the future.

---

## Absolute Red Line

**Ratis is never partisan.**

The prices displayed are real prices. No store, no brand, no commercial partner can pay to be ranked higher or promoted. Regardless of commercial pressure or financial opportunity — this line will never be crossed.

This is what makes Ratis credible. This is what makes users trust it.

---

## The Ideal User

A user who logs in every day, completes their daily missions, scans 1 to 2 receipts per week, enters 4 to 5 prices via in-store labels, and corrects errors on product pages.

They have fun. They save money. They contribute without realising it.

---

## 3-Year Success

Mass adoption. People love the product, use it every day, and recommend it to others. They have fun while saving money.

Ratis has become a reflex before grocery shopping — like checking the weather before going out.
